Flatbed Van

A flatbed truck is a type of truck which can be either articulated or rigid. As the name suggests, its bodywork is just an entirely flat, level 'bed' with no sides or roof. It's used to carry small loads, motocycles, car, etc..



Log Carrier

A logging truck or timber lorry is a large truck used to carry logs. Some have integrated flatbeds, some are discrete tractor units, and some are configured to spread a load between the tractor unit and a dollied trailer pulled behind it.



Semi Trailer

A semi-trailer truck, also known as a semitruck, is the combination of a tractor unit and one or more semi-trailers to carry freight. A semi-trailer for carrying the cargos on conatiners



18 Wheeler

18-wheeler refers to the combination of a semi-truck and semi-trailer. When connected they form a tractor-trailer unit, also referred to as an 18-wheeler. A 18 Wheeler for carrying the lengthy and larger cargos, etc..



Mini truck

Mini truck, also called a micro-truck, are tiny but practical light trucks, available in RWD or 4WD version. They are typically used on farms or by smaller business to transport light cargo.



Box Truck

It's a chassis cab truck with an enclosed cuboid-shaped cargo area. On most box trucks, the cabin is separate to the cargo area. The best freight uses for a box truck include less-than-truckload (LTL) freight. A box trucks used for parcel delivery, furniture hauling and perishable food delivery.



Mobile Crane

A mobile crane is a cable-controlled crane mounted on crawlers or rubber-tired carriers or a hydraulic-powered crane with a telescoping boom mounted on truck-type carriers or as self-propelled models. A mobile crane is an industrial piece of equipment used to easily transport cargo and building materials.
