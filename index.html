About Us

GR GROUPS is a highly diversified conglomerate with successful business entities in strategic locations INDIA. Founded by the acclaimed business visionary G. Tamil Selvan. GR is the principal investment holding company and promoter. Sixty-six percent of the equity share capital of GR is held by philanthropic trusts, which support education, health, livelihood generation and art and culture. GR has been our strategic customer.
